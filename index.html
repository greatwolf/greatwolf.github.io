The Bitcoin Faucet List

What is a faucet?

A faucet is a site where you can earn free bitcoins in exchange for advertising exposure or for completing small tasks. Faucets are a way for newcomers to get the feeling of earning, moving and spending bitcoins for free, even if in small amounts, so that they can try the system and, hopefully, get more involved with it.

Remember 1 Satoshi = 0.00000001 Bitcoin.

Why a bitcoin faucet list?

Earning bitcoins from faucets is an extremely simple business: search a faucet, visit the site, fill a captcha, wait a timeout, rinse and repeat. This is tedious work at best, especially if you are not using a faucet list that tracks your progress.

There are a few faucet lists available on the Internet, but you usually have to fight with popups, frames or just disorganized data that are not helping you in the the way it should.

This growing list of 254 faucets hopes to fullfill these needs: to be valuable, enjoyable and modern.
